HUMAN HEALTH AND DISEASE

IMMUNITY

Response shown by our body againsgt any antigen that invade our body is known as immunity.
There are two types of IMMUNITY
  1. Innate Immunity
  2. Aquired Immunity

Innate Immunity

Immune response in the form of barriers that are present since birth of an individual.
They can be of 4 types:
  1. Physical Barrier
    • Skin
    • Mucosal lining
  2. Cellular Barrier
    • Phagocytic Cell
      • Macrophages
      Monocytes
      Neutrophils
    • Non Phagocytic Cell
      • NK Cells (Natural Killer Cells)
  3. Physiological barrier
    • Fever
    • Saliva in mouth
    • HCL in stomach
      • ect.
  4. Cytokine Barrier
    5. Virus infected cell secrete protein called INTERFERONS, which protect non infected cell from further viral infections.
    They are made up of 270 amino acids.
    It stimulates adjacent cells to produce TRANSLATION INHIBITING PROTIEN(T.I.P).
    It is species specific.
    It is an anti-viral protien.
